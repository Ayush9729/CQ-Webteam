Favourite Places in IIT Kharagpur



NEHRU MUSEUM

This is without a doubt, IIT Kharagpur's centre of attraction. Now referred to as the Nehru Museum, this used to be IIT Kharagpur's old main building.


NALANDA COMPLEX

This is the Classroom complex of IIT Kharagpur, named after the iconic Nalanda University. It has a huge number of classrooms and houses the office of the CDC.


GOKHALE HALL

This is one of the oldest hostels of IIT Kharagpur. Presently, it consists of around 180 first year undergraduate students
